Welcome to Flight Chalker! 🎯
Here's a quick guide to get you started
📋
Record Match
After each match, go to Record Match and enter the results for each single, double and The Gallon. Hit Save & Submit when done.
📅
Fixtures
View upcoming fixtures in the Fixtures tab. Click Record Results to load a fixture into the match form automatically.
📊
History & Stats
View past results in History. Download match PDFs or edit results. Check individual player stats in the Stats tab.
👥
Squad
Manage your players in the Players tab. Share your team join code from the Squad tab so players can link their accounts.
💾
Save Draft
Use Save Draft during a match to save progress. Next time you log in, you'll be offered to restore where you left off.
